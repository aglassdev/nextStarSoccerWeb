## Design Context

### Users
Internal admins at Next Star Soccer — coaches, coordinators, and org staff managing players, billing, events, and inquiries. Used daily at a desk, in a focused work context.

### Brand Personality
Professional, calm, authoritative. Three words: **focused · warm · precise**.

### Aesthetic Direction
Warm dark monochrome — near-black backgrounds tinted warm brown (#0d0b09), surfaces in charcoal-brown (#111009), text in warm off-white (#e8e2d9). No color accents or colored badges anywhere. Active states use a subtly lighter warm background. Charts use tonal warm grays. The feel is like a well-designed internal tool at a premium organization — not cold/tech, not sporty.

**Anti-references:** Cold blue-black dashboards, cyan/neon accents, colored status badges, glassmorphism.

### Design Principles
1. **No color spots** — status, badges, and active states must use warm grays only, never hues.
2. **Warm over cold** — all darks lean brown/amber, never pure black or cool gray.
3. **Typography-first hierarchy** — distinguish importance through weight and opacity, not color.
4. **Calm density** — information-rich but never cluttered; generous vertical rhythm.
5. **Monochrome charts** — bar and donut charts use tonal warm grays, not colored segments.
